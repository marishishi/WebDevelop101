Who is this panda lover?

Hello! I would like to introduce myself. My name is Mari and I am a human being. I was born and raised in Japan. I currently live in Kyoto, a city where we do not have a panda. What a shame! I am very happy when I am wearing black and white becuase I feel like I'm a panda.

Facts about pandas...

  • They spend most of the time eating when they are awake.
  • They can eat chocolate.
  • Their tails are white, not black.
  • They are good at climbing trees.
  • They love to play with humans.
